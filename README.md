## Quick Overview

A football principle — play the ball, not the player — unpacked as a life framework across five domains:

1. **Disagreements and debate** — attack the idea, not the person
2. **Feedback and growth** — describe behavior, not character
3. **Relationships** — solve the problem, don't indict the partner
4. **Career and ambition** — outwork, don't undermine
5. **Leadership** — build psychological safety through ball-first culture

Includes a practical toolkit (5 techniques), psychological foundations (why we default to the player), and the philosophical underpinnings (Stoicism, game theory, ad hominem taxonomy).
