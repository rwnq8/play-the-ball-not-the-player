## Read the Flagship Piece

> **Start here → [Ball First, Person Never](blog-post)**

*"The stadium roars. A winger sprints down the touchline, ball glued to his feet. The defender closes in — and here is where it all divides..."*

The flagship blog post carries this metaphor across five domains:

1. **Disagreements & Debate** — Attack the idea, not the person
2. **Feedback & Growth** — Describe behavior, not character
3. **Relationships** — Solve the problem, don't indict the partner
4. **Career & Ambition** — Outwork, don't undermine
5. **Leadership** — Build psychological safety through ball-first culture

Includes a practical toolkit with five techniques you can use today.
